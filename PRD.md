# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Angela Maranto is a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa, graduating in May 2026. She is targeting Business Analyst roles at Fortune 500 companies, with a focus on tech companies in Chicago.

While her resume outlines her experience and skills, it does not fully capture her personality, technical capabilities, and project work in an engaging and accessible format. This landing page will serve as a centralized digital presence that allows recruiters and hiring managers to quickly understand who she is, what she has done, and where she is headed professionally.

---

## 02 — Target Audience

The primary audience for this landing page includes:

- Recruiters hiring for Business Analyst roles at Fortune 500 companies
- Hiring managers in tech companies located in Chicago
- Professionals reviewing candidates for internships or full-time roles in analytics, consulting, or business-related positions

These visitors are looking for:
- A quick understanding of Angela’s background and career direction
- Evidence of technical skills (SQL, Python, data analysis)
- Examples of real projects and applied work
- Easy ways to contact or connect (LinkedIn, GitHub, email)

---

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|------|------------------|
| Increase visibility to recruiters | Number of website visits |
| Generate interview opportunities | Number of interviews scheduled |
| Support job applications | Number of applications submitted |
| Improve professional presence | Increased engagement on LinkedIn/GitHub |
